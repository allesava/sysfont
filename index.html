Sysfont

Revival of Susan Kare’s Chicago font from the first versions of MacOS (then called System Software).

Download Sysfont

Technical Details

Drawn as pixel art in Illustrator and exported with FontSelf. Diacritic marks and symbols were complete guesswork, so some differences from the original are possible.
